## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the Talenti homepage
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Choose your account type:
   - **Candidate**: Looking for jobs
   - **Organisation**: Hiring talent
4. Enter your email and create a password
5. Verify your email (if required)

### Logging In

1. Click **Sign In**
2. Enter your email and password
3. You'll be directed to your dashboard

## For Candidates

### Setting Up Your Profile

A complete profile helps employers understand your qualifications.

#### Personal Information
1. Navigate to **Profile**
2. Fill in your details:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Phone number
   - Location
   - LinkedIn profile (optional)
   - Portfolio URL (optional)

#### Uploading Your CV/Resume
1. Click **Upload CV**
2. Select a PDF or Word document
3. Talenti will automatically extract:
   - Work experience
   - Education
   - Skills

**Tip:** Review the extracted information and make corrections if needed.

#### Adding Skills
1. Go to **Skills** section
2. Click **Add Skill**
3. Enter skill name (e.g., "Python", "Project Management")
4. Select proficiency level

#### Work Experience
1. Go to **Experience** section
2. Click **Add Experience**
3. Fill in:
   - Company name
   - Job title
   - Start/end dates
   - Description of responsibilities

### Practice Interviews

Before your real interview, practice with our AI interviewer.

1. From your dashboard, click **Practice Interview**
2. Select a practice category:
   - General
   - Technical
   - Behavioural
3. Click **Start Practice**
4. Allow microphone and camera access
5. The AI will ask you questions
6. Speak clearly into your microphone
7. When finished, you'll receive feedback

**Tips for Practice:**
- Find a quiet, well-lit space
- Test your audio before starting
- Practice answering out loud
- Review feedback to improve

### Taking a Real Interview

When you're invited to interview:

1. Check your email for the invitation
2. Click the **Start Interview** link
3. You'll see the interview lobby with:
   - Company name
   - Role title
   - Interview duration estimate

**Before Starting:**
- [ ] Find a quiet location
- [ ] Ensure stable internet connection
- [ ] Test your camera and microphone
- [ ] Close other applications
- [ ] Have water nearby

**During the Interview:**

1. Click **Begin Interview**
2. The AI interviewer will introduce itself
3. Answer each question clearly
4. Take your time to think before answering
5. The AI will ask follow-up questions
6. After 5-6 questions, the interview will conclude

**After the Interview:**

1. You'll see a completion confirmation
2. The recording is processed (if applicable)
3. Results are shared with the employer
4. You may receive feedback

### Managing Privacy

Control what employers can see:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Privacy**
2. Choose visibility options:
   - **Full Profile**: Employers see everything
   - **Limited**: Only basic info shown
   - **Hidden**: Pause your job search

### Requesting Data Deletion

Under GDPR, you can request deletion of your data:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Data & Privacy**
2. Click **Request Deletion**
3. Choose deletion type:
   - Delete everything
   - Delete recordings only
   - Anonymize data
4. Confirm your request

## For Recruiters

### Organisation Setup

If you're setting up a new organisation:

1. Complete onboarding after signup
2. Enter company details:
   - Company name
   - Industry
   - Website
   - Description
3. Add company values (used for culture fit scoring)

### Creating Job Roles

1. Click **New Role** from your dashboard
2. Fill in role details:
   - Title
   - Department
   - Location
   - Work type (remote/hybrid/onsite)
   - Employment type
   - Salary range (optional)

3. Add job description
4. Click **Extract Requirements** to have AI identify:
   - Required skills
   - Experience requirements
   - Qualifications
   - Suggested interview questions

5. Review and edit as needed
6. Set role status:
   - **Draft**: Not visible
   - **Active**: Accepting applications
   - **Paused**: Temporarily hidden
   - **Closed**: No longer accepting

### Customizing Scoring Rubric

Adjust how candidates are evaluated:

1. Open the role → **Edit Rubric**
2. Modify dimension weights:
   - Technical Skills (default 20%)
   - Communication (default 15%)
   - Culture Fit (default 10%)
   - etc.
3. Total must equal 100%
4. Save changes

### Inviting Candidates

When you're ready to interview a candidate:

1. Open the candidate's application
2. Click **Send Interview Invitation**
3. Confirm email address
4. Set expiration (default 7 days)
5. Click **Send**

The candidate will receive an email with a unique interview link.

### Generating Shortlists

Use AI to rank candidates:

1. From the role page, click **Generate Shortlist**
2. AI analyzes all applicants against requirements
3. Candidates are scored 0-100
4. View match reasons for each candidate
5. Compare candidates side-by-side

### Reviewing Interviews

After candidates complete interviews:

1. Go to **Interviews** tab
2. Click on a completed interview
3. View:
   - Overall score
   - Dimension breakdown
   - AI narrative summary
   - Candidate feedback
   - Full transcript
   - Recording (if available)

### Exporting Reports

Generate PDF reports for hiring committees:

1. Open the interview report
2. Click **Export PDF**
3. Choose sections to include
4. Download report

## For Administrators

### Team Management

Add team members to your organisation:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Team**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter email address
4. Assign role:
   - **Admin**: Full access
   - **Recruiter**: Manage roles and interviews
   - **Viewer**: Read-only access
5. Send invitation

### Organisation Settings

Configure company-wide settings:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Organisation**
2. Update:
   - Company logo
   - Description
   - Values framework
   - Billing information

### Recording Retention

Set how long interview recordings are kept:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Data Retention**
2. Set retention period (default 60 days)
3. Recordings are automatically deleted after this period

### Audit Logs

View activity history:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Audit Log**
2. Filter by:
   - Date range
   - User
   - Action type
3. Export for compliance

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Is Talenti free to use?**
A: Talenti offers both free and paid plans. Contact us for pricing.

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Edge, and Firefox. Safari has limited support.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. We use encryption, secure authentication, and follow GDPR guidelines.

### For Candidates

**Q: Can I redo my interview?**
A: This depends on the employer's settings. Contact them directly.

**Q: How long are interviews?**
A: Typically 10-15 minutes for AI interviews.

**Q: What if I have technical issues?**
A: Refresh the page. If problems persist, contact support.

**Q: Will the AI judge my accent?**
A: No. The AI evaluates content, not accent or speech patterns.

### For Recruiters

**Q: How accurate is AI scoring?**
A: AI scoring provides consistent evaluation. Human review is recommended for final decisions.

**Q: Can I edit AI scores?**
A: Yes. Admins can override scores with documented reasoning.

**Q: How do I delete a role?**
A: Close the role first, then contact support for deletion.

## Troubleshooting

### Camera/Microphone Not Working

1. Check browser permissions (click lock icon in URL bar)
2. Ensure no other app is using the camera
3. Try a different browser
4. Restart your device

### Interview Won't Start

1. Check your internet connection
2. Clear browser cache
3. Disable browser extensions
4. Try incognito/private mode

### Invitation Link Expired

Contact the recruiter who sent the invitation to request a new link.

### Audio Quality Issues

1. Use headphones to prevent echo
2. Move closer to your microphone
3. Close other applications
4. Check internet bandwidth

### Video Freezing

1. Reduce video quality if available
2. Move closer to your router
3. Close background applications
4. Try a wired connection

### Login Issues

1. Check email and password
2. Try password reset
3. Check for caps lock
4. Clear cookies and try again
